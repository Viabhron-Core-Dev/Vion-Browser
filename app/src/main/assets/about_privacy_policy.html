Vion Browser — Privacy Policy

Zero Telemetry

Vion Browser collects no data whatsoever. There are no analytics, no crash reporters, no usage statistics, and no external connections made by the browser itself.

All Data Stays On Device

All browsing history, bookmarks, saved pages, and AI conversation history are stored exclusively on your device. No data is ever uploaded to any server operated by Vion Browser.

AI Assistant

The AI assistant only sends data to the AI provider you explicitly configure and choose. API keys are stored using Android EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256-GCM). Conversation history is stored in a local Room database and never uploaded.

Web Browsing

When you browse the web, your requests go directly to the websites you visit. Vion Browser does not proxy, monitor, or log your web traffic.

Fork Attribution

Vion Browser is based on Privacy Browser by Soren Stoutner and the monocles browser fork. The original privacy policy of Privacy Browser applies to the upstream code. Vion's additions maintain the same or stronger privacy standards.

Open Source

Vion Browser is open source (GPL v3). You can inspect exactly what it does at any time.
